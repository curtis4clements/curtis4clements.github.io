Open-Learning Collective: A List of Educational Resources

Education has gotten expensive, VERY expensive! I've been attempting to create a reference document to allow for exploration of FREE educational resources. Organized below by category are websites that can be explored below.


Below are different educational resources organized by their subject matter.

Computer Science

  • MIT OPEN COURSEWARE - https://www.ocw.mit.edu

    • Open education resource spanning multiple subjects, but specializes in technology


  • W3SCHOOLS - https://www.w3schools.com

    • Comprehensive and frequently updated resource for web design. Sets a golden standard as an asset.


  • CODECADEMY - https://www.codecademy.com

    • Hands on guide to coding. Offers very good selection of languages to learn from


Information Technology

  • Microsoft Learn - https://learn.microsoft.com/

    • Microsoft documentation along with certifications


General / How-To's

  • YOUTUBE - https://www.youtube.com/

    • Biggest repository of videos on the internet. Plenty of subjects are easily accessible by a multitude of creators


  • INTERNET ARCHIVE - https://archive.org/

    • An amazing collection of different books and entertainment. While not limited to educational resources, this contains firsthand records of media throughout the internet age.


  • THE SPRUCE CRAFTS - https://www.thesprucecrafts.com/

    • All-ages guides/resource for sewing, knitting, and other handcrafts
